Na Di 邸娜

I am a Ph.D. student in the Data Science and Analytics Thrust at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), advised by Prof. Jiaheng Wei. My research focuses on data-centric AI for foundation models — improving the quality of training data and detecting errors in AI-generated annotations.

Email · Google Scholar · GitHub

Research Interests

  • Data-centric AI for foundation models: improving data quality for training large language and multimodal models.
  • Reliable AI-generated annotation: detecting errors in AI-generated annotations.
  • Human–AI data curation: combining automatic model annotation with expert supervision.
